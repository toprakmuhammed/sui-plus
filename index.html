How does renting an item work?
Select an item in Rent, choose Rent, confirm on-chain, and the item becomes usable instantly for the selected duration. It returns automatically when time ends.
What are the fees?
Listings show a daily price in SUI. Network fees apply per on-chain action. There‚Äôs no hidden platform fee in the MVP.
Can I cancel a rental early?
Early returns are not supported in MVP. The item auto-returns at the end of the rental period.
How do I list my own item?
Use List your item in the Rent toolbar. Choose the game, set the daily price and duration, then confirm on-chain.
